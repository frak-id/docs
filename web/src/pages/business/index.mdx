# What is Frak Ads Manager?

Frak Ads Manager is your starting point for running word-of-mouth ad campaigns on your website. It’s an all-in-one tool for creating campaigns, managing when and where they’ll run, and tracking how well your campaigns are performing towards your marketing goals.

With the Frak Ads Manager dashboard, you can monitor your campaigns in real time. You’ll have the flexibility to create and edit campaigns, track their performance, and manage ad budgets and schedules with ease.

Frak empowers businesses by optimizing word-of-mouth advertising, ensuring that your customers become advocates, amplifying your brand’s reach through real user interactions.
